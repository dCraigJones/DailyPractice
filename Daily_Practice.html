Notes

Look for the CAT:
  • (C) Checks
  • (A) Attacks
  • (T) Tactics
When in check you can:
  • (A) run Away
  • (B) Block
  • (C) Capture
When attacked you can:
  • (A) run Away
  • (B) Block
  • (C) Capture
  • (D) Defend the piece
  • (E) Equal (attack a more valuable piece)
  • (F) Fix (pin the attacker so it can't move)
  • or Do Nothing
